Facilities and Activities

Pantai Karnaval Ancol offers a wide range of facilities and activities to cater to the diverse needs and interests of its visitors. Here are some of the top highlights:

  1. Beachside Cafe: Enjoy a refreshing meal or snack while taking in the breathtaking views of the ocean.
  2. Water Sports: Try your hand at surfing, swimming, or kayaking for an adrenaline-packed experience.
  3. Game Station: Challenge your friends and family to a friendly game of table tennis, pool, or arcade games.
  4. Children's Playground: Let your little ones enjoy the colorful playground equipment and kid-friendly activities.
  5. Fitness Station: Get fit and energized with a variety of exercise equipment and fitness classes.
  6. Food and Beverage: Savor a delicious meal at one of the many food stalls or restaurants on the premises.
  7. Area Broadcasting: Catch the live music performances or sports events on the giant screen.

Tips and Reminders

Before you plan your visit to Pantai Karnaval Ancol, here are a few tips and reminders to keep in mind:

  1. Dress Code: Wear comfortable and casual clothing, with sunscreen and a hat to protect yourself from the sun.
  2. Arrival Time: Plan your arrival at least 30 minutes before the beach opens to beat the crowds.
  3. Ticket Redemption: Present your e-ticket or printed ticket at the entrance to avoid any inconvenience.
  4. Parking: Designate parking areas are available, but be prepared for peak-hour congestion.
  5. Respect the Environment: Help keep the beach clean by throwing away your trash and respecting the marine life.
