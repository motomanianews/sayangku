If you're planning to catch a movie at Dinoyo XXI, you might be wondering what the ticket prices are. In this article, we'll explore the current ticket prices for XXI cinemas at Dinoyo Mall Malang.

How to Buy Tickets

Before we dive into the ticket prices, let's go through the process of buying tickets at Dinoyo XXI. You have several options to choose from:

  • Online Booking: You can purchase tickets through the XXI website or mobile app. This is the most convenient way to buy tickets, as you can do it from the comfort of your own home.
  • Ticket Counter: If you prefer to buy tickets in person, you can visit the ticket counter at Dinoyo Mall. This option is available during mall operating hours.
  • Mobile App: The XXI mobile app allows you to buy tickets and even reserve seats. This feature is available for select movies and is only available on the app.

Ticket Prices

Now that we've covered how to buy tickets, let's explore the current ticket prices for XXI cinemas at Dinoyo Mall Malang. The prices vary depending on the time of day, movie format, and seat selection. Here are the current ticket prices:

  • 2D Movie: The 2D movie ticket price at Dinoyo XXI ranges from IDR 80,000 to IDR 150,000 per person, depending on the time of day.
  • 3D Movie: The 3D movie ticket price at Dinoyo XXI ranges from IDR 120,000 to IDR 200,000 per person, depending on the time of day.
  • Gold Class: The Gold Class ticket price at Dinoyo XXI ranges from IDR 250,000 to IDR 400,000 per person, depending on the time of day. Gold Class tickets come with additional perks, such as reclining seats and personalized service.
  • ScreenX: The ScreenX ticket price at Dinoyo XXI ranges from IDR 280,000 to IDR 450,000 per person, depending on the time of day. ScreenX is a unique movie format that uses a 270-degree screen to create an immersive viewing experience.
