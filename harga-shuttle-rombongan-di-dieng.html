Harga Shuttle Rombongan Di Dieng

Admin November 12, 2024

Harga Shuttle Rombongan Di Dieng - Located in the heart of Central Java, Dieng is a popular tourist destination known for its natural beauty, rich history, and cultural heritage. With its stunning scenery, Buddhist temples, and clear sky, Dieng is a perfect getaway for nature lovers and adventure seekers. To make your trip to Dieng even more enjoyable, booking a shuttle service for your group is highly recommended. In this article, we will discuss the prices and types of group shuttle services available in Dieng, as well as some tips on how to choose the right service for your needs.

Harga Shuttle Rombongan di Dieng

The prices of group shuttle services in Dieng vary depending on the type of vehicle, route, and number of passengers. On average, the cost of a one-way trip for a group of 10-15 people can range from IDR 1,500,000 to IDR 2,500,000(around USD 100-180). For a round-trip journey, the cost can range from IDR 2,500,000 to IDR 4,000,000(around USD 180-280).

Here are some examples of group shuttle services available in Dieng, along with their prices:

  • Dieng Tour: This service offers a one-day trip to Dieng from Yogyakarta, including transportation, entrance fees, and a guide. The price for a group of 10-15 people is IDR 2,200,000 (around USD 150).
  • Jogja Dieng: This service offers a round-trip journey to Dieng from Yogyakarta, including transportation and entrance fees. The price for a group of 10-15 people is IDR 3,500,000 (around USD 240).
  • Dieng Express: This is a budget-friendly option that offers a one-way trip to Dieng from Yogyakarta, including transportation and entrance fees. The price for a group of 10-15 people is IDR 1,800,000 (around USD 120).

Tips Mencari Shuttle Rombongan di Dieng

Choosing the right group shuttle service in Dieng can be overwhelming, especially for tourists who are not familiar with the area. Here are some tips to help you make the right decision:

  • Decide on your budget: Set a budget for your group and stick to it. Research the prices of different services and choose the one that fits your budget.
  • Check the route: Make sure the service covers the route you need. Some services may only offer transportation to specific areas in Dieng, so check the route before booking.
  • Check the type of vehicle: Consider the type of vehicle you need. If you have a large group, you may need a minivan or a coach, while a small group can be transported in a sedan or a minibus.
  • Check the service's reputation: Research the service's reputation online and read reviews from other customers. This can give you an idea of the level of service you can expect.

Tips Perjalanan Ke Dieng

Here are some tips to help you make the most of your trip to Dieng:

  • Plan ahead: Book your shuttle service in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak season.
  • Pack wisely: Pack lightly and bring essentials such as water, snacks, and sun protection.
  • Bring a guidebook: Bring a guidebook or a map of Dieng to help you navigate the area and plan your itinerary.
  • Enjoy the scenery: Take time to appreciate the stunning scenery and natural beauty of Dieng. There are many viewpoints and vantage points that offer breathtaking views of the surrounding landscape.

In conclusion, booking a shuttle service for your group in Dieng can be a convenient and cost-effective way to get around the area. By considering the prices, types of vehicles, and services available, you can make the right decision for your group. Additionally, by following the tips provided in this article, you can make the most of your trip to Dieng and enjoy the natural beauty and cultural heritage of this popular tourist destination.
