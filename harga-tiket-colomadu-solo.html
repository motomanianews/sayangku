Harga Tiket Colomadu Solo

Admin November 24, 2024

Harga Tiket Colomadu Solo - Solo, a city steeped in history and culture, is a must-visit destination for anyone interested in exploring Indonesia's rich heritage. And what better way to experience the city than by taking a trip to Kolomadu, a beautiful and historic mosque located in the heart of Solo? In this article, we will guide you on how to get to Kolomadu, the various ways to visit, and most importantly, the prices of ticket to get you there.

Getting to Solo

Before we dive into the details of Kolomadu, let's talk about how to get to Solo. Solo is located in the Central Java province of Indonesia, and it is easily accessible by air, land, or sea. Here are some options for getting to Solo:

By Air

The Adisucipto International Airport(SOC) is the main airport serving Solo, and it is located about 15 kilometers from the city center. You can fly to Solo from major cities in Indonesia such as Jakarta, Yogyakarta, and Bali. From the airport, you can take a taxi, rental car, or public transportation to get to your hotel.

By Land

Solo is also easily accessible by land transportation. The city is connected to other major cities in Indonesia by bus and train. You can take a bus from Jakarta or Yogyakarta to Solo, or take a train from Jakarta or Bandung to Solo. The bus and train station are located in the city center, and you can get to your hotel from there.

By Sea

If you are coming from the city of Semarang, you can take a ferry to Solo. The ferry port is located in the city center, and you can get to your hotel from there.

Visiting Kolomadu

Kolomadu is located in the heart of Solo, and it is easily accessible by public transportation or on foot. Here are some steps to follow to visit Kolomadu:

  1. Take a taxi or rental car from your hotel to the Kolomadu complex, which is located on Jalan Kolomadu, Solo.
  2. You can also take a public bus from the city center to Kolomadu. The bus stop is located near the complex.
  3. Once you arrive at the complex, you can walk to the mosque, which is located in the center of the complex.
  4. You can take a guided tour of the mosque, which will give you a deeper understanding of its history and architecture.

Harga Tiket Kolomadu

The ticket price for visiting Kolomadu varies depending on the type of ticket you purchase. Here are the ticket prices:

Regular Ticket

The regular ticket price for visiting Kolomadu is IDR 10,000 per person. This ticket allows you to visit the mosque and take a guided tour.

VIP Ticket

The VIP ticket price for visiting Kolomadu is IDR 25,000 per person. This ticket allows you to visit the mosque and take a guided tour, as well as access to the VIP lounge.

Family Ticket

The family ticket price for visiting Kolomadu is IDR 50,000 per family(up to 4 people). This ticket allows you to visit the mosque and take a guided tour, as well as access to the VIP lounge.

Group Ticket

The group ticket price for visiting Kolomadu is IDR 100,000 per group(up to 10 people). This ticket allows you to visit the mosque and take a guided tour, as well as access to the VIP lounge.

Tips for Visiting Kolomadu

Here are some tips for visiting Kolomadu:

  1. Dress modestly, as the mosque is a place of worship for the local community.
  2. Remove your shoes before entering the mosque.
  3. Do not touch or disturb the decorations or artifacts in the mosque.
  4. Respect the local culture and traditions of the mosque.
  5. Take your time and enjoy the beauty of the mosque and its surroundings.

In conclusion, visiting Kolomadu is a must-do experience when in Solo. With its beautiful architecture, rich history, and stunning design, it is a place that is sure to leave a lasting impression on visitors. By following the steps outlined in this article, you can ensure a memorable and enjoyable visit to Kolomadu.
