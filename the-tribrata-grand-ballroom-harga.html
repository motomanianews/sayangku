About the Tibrata Grand Ballroom

The Tibrata Grand Ballroom is a stunning function space that can accommodate up to 500 guests. The ballroom features a sleek and modern design, complete with a spacious dance floor, lavish chandeliers, and state-of-the-art sound and lighting systems. Whether you're planning an intimate gathering or a grand affair, the Tibrata Grand Ballroom has everything you need to make your special day unforgettable.

Harga: What You Can Expect

So, what's the harga of this luxurious wedding venue? At the Tibrata Grand Ballroom, our prices vary depending on the day of the week, season, and number of guests. Generally, our harga ranges from IDR 10,000,000 to IDR 50,000,000 per event. Here are some estimated costs to give you a better idea of what to expect:

  • IDR 10,000,000 for a weekday event with up to 100 guests
  • IDR 15,000,000 for a weekday event with up to 200 guests
  • IDR 20,000,000 for a weekend event with up to 100 guests
  • IDR 30,000,000 for a weekend event with up to 200 guests
  • IDR 50,000,000 for a grand event with up to 500 guests

Catering and Amenities

At the Tibrata Grand Ballroom, we understand the importance of delicious food and refreshing drinks to make your special day unforgettable. Our in-house catering team offers a range of menu options, from casual buffets to formal plated meals. We also provide a range of amenities to ensure your guests' comfort and convenience, including:

  • Complimentary parking for guests
  • By-pass on the main road for easy access
  • Ample restroom facilities
  • In-house sound and lighting systems
  • Projector and screen for presentations

Hotel Accommodations

The Tibrata Grand Ballroom offers on-site hotel accommodations for out-of-town guests, ensuring that everyone can enjoy a comfortable and relaxing stay close to the venue. Our rooms feature modern amenities, including air conditioning, flat-screen TVs, and complimentary Wi-Fi.
