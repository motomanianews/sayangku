Nasi Kalong Bandung Harga

Admin December 21, 2024

Nasi Kalong Bandung Harga - Bandung, city of roses, is a popular tourist destination known for its charming atmosphere, delicious food, and affordable prices. One of the must-try dishes in Bandung is Nasi Kalong, a signature dish that has gained popularity among locals and tourists alike. In this article, we'll explore the menu and pricing of Nasi Kalong Bandung, so you can plan your culinary adventure in this beautiful city.

Apa Itu Nasi Kalong?

Nasi Kalong is a traditional Indonesian dish that consists of a bed of rice topped with a variety of ingredients such as beef or chicken, vegetables, and spices. The name"Nasi Kalong" literally means"flying rice," which refers to the way the rice is served on a banana leaf, making it look like it's flying. This dish is a staple in Bandung and is often served for special occasions and celebrations.

Menu Nasi Kalong Bandung

Nasi Kalong Bandung has several variations, but the most popular ones are:

  • Nasi Kalong ISI: This is the most popular version of Nasi Kalong, which comes with a variety of ingredients such as beef or chicken, vegetables, and spices. The price range for this dish is IDR 20,000 to IDR 30,000.
  • Nasi Kalong KOBE: This version is similar to Nasi Kalong ISI, but it's served with Kobe-style beef or chicken. The price range for this dish is IDR 30,000 to IDR 50,000.
  • Nasi Kalong KHAS: This option is for those who want a unique twist on the traditional Nasi Kalong. It's served with a variety of seafood such as fish, shrimp, and squid. The price range for this dish is IDR 40,000 to IDR 60,000.

Harga Nasi Kalong Bandung

The price of Nasi Kalong Bandung varies depending on the location and the type of ingredients used. Here are some approximate prices for Nasi Kalong in Bandung:

  • Warung and Food Stalls: IDR 10,000 to IDR 20,000
  • Restaurants: IDR 20,000 to IDR 50,000
  • High-End Restaurants: IDR 50,000 to IDR 100,000

Tips for Trying Nasi Kalong Bandung

Before trying Nasi Kalong Bandung, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Choose a popular restaurant: Look for restaurants that are popular among locals and tourists alike. This ensures that you'll get a good quality dish.
  • Be patient: Nasi Kalong Bandung is a popular dish, so be prepared to wait in line or order in advance.
  • Order extra rice: Nasi Kalong Bandung is served with a small amount of rice, so consider ordering extra rice if you're hungry.
  • Don't forget to try the desserts: Many Nasi Kalong Bandung restaurants offer delicious desserts such as bakpia and es teler. Be sure to try them out!

Kelebihan Nasi Kalong Bandung

Nasi Kalong Bandung has several benefits that make it a must-try dish for anyone visiting Bandung. Here are some of the benefits:

  • Affordable: Nasi Kalong Bandung is an affordable dish that won't break the bank.
  • Delicious: Nasi Kalong Bandung is a delicious dish that's sure to satisfy your appetite.
  • Authentic: Nasi Kalong Bandung is an authentic Indonesian dish that's popular among locals and tourists alike.
  • Variety: Nasi Kalong Bandung offers a variety of options, so you're sure to find one that suits your taste and budget.

In conclusion, Nasi Kalong Bandung is a must-try dish for anyone visiting Bandung. With its delicious flavors, authentic ingredients, and affordable price, it's no wonder why Nasi Kalong Bandung is a popular dish among locals and tourists alike. Whether you're a foodie or just looking for a tasty meal, Nasi Kalong Bandung is a dish that's sure to satisfy your appetite.
