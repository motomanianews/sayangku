Harga Tiket CinemaXX Kendari

The ticket prices at CinemaXX Kendari vary depending on the type of seat and the showtime. Here are the current price ranges:

  • Economy Class: IDR 50,000 - IDR 70,000 (approximately USD 3.50 - USD 4.90)
  • Standard Class: IDR 70,000 - IDR 100,000 (approximately USD 4.90 - USD 6.80)
  • Premium Class: IDR 100,000 - IDR 150,000 (approximately USD 6.80 - USD 10.20)
  • VIP Class: IDR 150,000 - IDR 200,000 (approximately USD 10.20 - USD 13.70)

Tips for Getting the Best Seats

When it comes to getting the best seats at CinemaXX Kendari, here are some tips to keep in mind:

  • Arrive early: Get to the cinema at least 30 minutes before showtime to secure a good seat.
  • Choose the middle seats: The middle seats in the row tend to have the best view and are less likely to be disturbed.
  • Avoid the front seats: The front seats are often reserved for VIP customers, and you may not get the best view from that distance.
  • Consider the Premium Class: The Premium Class seats offer more legroom and a wider screen, making them a great option for an immersive experience.

How to Get to CinemaXX Kendari

CinemaXX Kendari is located in the heart of Kendari, making it easily accessible by public transportation or private vehicle. Here are the directions:

  • By Public Transportation: Take bus number 1 or 5 from the Kendari City Bus Terminal and get off at the CinemaXX Kendari stop.
  • By Private Vehicle: Drive from the Kendari City Center to the CinemaXX Kendari location, which takes approximately 10-15 minutes.

Amenities and Services

CinemaXX Kendari offers a range of amenities and services to make your movie-going experience more enjoyable. Here are some of the services they offer:

  • Food and Beverage: Enjoy a wide range of snacks and drinks at the cinema's concession stand.
  • Wi-Fi: Stay connected with your friends and family while you're at the cinema with their free Wi-Fi service.
  • Parking: The cinema offers a parking lot for customers, but it's limited to only 20 vehicles.

Conclusion

CinemaXX Kendari is a popular destination for movie enthusiasts in Southeast Sulawesi, offering a wide range of movie genres and a state-of-the-art facility. Whether you're a local or a tourist, CinemaXX Kendari is definitely worth a visit.
