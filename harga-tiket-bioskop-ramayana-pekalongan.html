Ticket Prices for a Blissful Afternoon at Ramayana Pekalongan

Ramayana Pekalongan offers a wide range of ticket options to cater to different tastes and budgets. Whether you're a film buff, a family with kids, or a group of friends, there's a ticket package that suits your needs. Here's a breakdown of the ticket prices at Ramayana Pekalongan:

Regular Ticket

The regular ticket price at Ramayana Pekalongan starts from IDR 80,000 for 2D movies. For 3D movies, the ticket price is ranging from IDR 120,000 to IDR 150,000. The ticket prices may vary depending on the time of day, with Saturday and Sunday shows typically priced higher than weekday shows.

Premium Ticket

For a more luxurious experience, Ramayana Pekalongan offers premium tickets starting from IDR 200,000 for 2D movies. Premium tickets grant access to special services like reclining seats, priority entry, and complimentary snacks. For 3D movies, the premium ticket price ranges from IDR 250,000 to IDR 300,000.

Family Pack

Ramayana Pekalongan also offers family packages for families with kids. The family pack includes 4 tickets and a set of snacks starting from IDR 400,000. This is an excellent value for families who want to spend a fun day out together.

Student and Senior Discounts

Ramayana Pekalongan offers discounts for students and senior citizens. Students with a valid student ID can enjoy a 10% discount on regular tickets, while senior citizens can get a 15% discount.
