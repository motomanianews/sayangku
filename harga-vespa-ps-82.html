Harga Vespa Ps 82 - In the world of scooters, few names evoke the same level of nostalgia and excitement as Vespa. The Italian brand has been a staple of European streets for decades, and one of its most iconic models is the PS 82. If you're in the market for a Vespa PS 82, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll delve into the history of the scooter, its features, and most importantly, the current market price.

A Brief History of the Vespa PS 82

Launched in the 1950s, the Vespa PS 82 was one of the first street-legal scooters designed by Corradino D'Ascanio. This innovative model revolutionized the way people commuted, offering a stylish, practical, and affordable mode of transportation. The PS 82 quickly gained popularity across Europe and beyond, with its sleek design, reliability, and ease of use appealing to a wide range of riders.

Features of the Vespa PS 82

The Vespa PS 82 is a testament to timeless design and engineering. Some of its notable features include:

  • Iconic Styling: The PS 82's retro-futuristic design has been refined over the years, yet still maintains its original charm.
  • 1-Cylinder, 98cc Engine: This compact engine provides a smooth and quiet ride, making it perfect for city commuting.
  • 4-Stroke, Air-Cooled: The engine's air-cooling system ensures a reliable and consistent performance.
  • 4-Speed Manual Transmission: Riders have more control over their ride with this user-friendly transmission.
  • 120mm Drum Brake: The PS 82's drum brake provides reliable stopping power, ensuring a safe and secure ride.

Current Market Price of the Vespa PS 82

As with any vintage or classic vehicle, the price of a Vespa PS 82 can vary greatly depending on factors such as condition, year, and location. Here are some general price ranges to expect:

  • Restored Models: £4,000 - £8,000 (approximately IDR 70,000,000 - IDR 140,000,000)
  • Good Condition Models: £2,500 - £4,000 (approximately IDR 45,000,000 - IDR 70,000,000)
  • Average Condition Models: £1,500 - £2,500 (approximately IDR 25,000,000 - IDR 45,000,000)
  • Restoration Projects: £1,000 - £1,500 (approximately IDR 18,000,000 - IDR 25,000,000)

Keep in mind that these prices are only estimates and may vary depending on the specific scooter and market conditions.

Conclusion

The Vespa PS 82 is a true classic, combining style, functionality, and a touch of nostalgia. Whether you're a seasoned Vespa enthusiast or a newcomer to the world of scooters, the PS 82 is an excellent choice. With its unbeatable charm, reliable performance, and surprisingly affordable price tag, this iconic scooter is sure to capture your heart.
