Spek Dan Harga Note 5 -

Spek Dan Harga Note 5 - Samsung Galaxy Note 5 adalah salah satu flagship terbaik Samsung pada tahun 2015. Note 5 hadir dengan spesifikasi teknis yang luar biasa dan fitur-fitur yang menjadikannya salah satu ponsel cerdas terbaik saat itu. Dalam artikel ini, kita akan mencari tahu tentang spek dan harga Note 5, serta memberikan analisis detail tentang kelebihan dan kekurangan perangkat ini.

Spesifikasi Teknis

Prosesor dan RAM

Note 5 didukung oleh prosesor Exynos 7420 octa-core dengan clock speed 1.8 GHz dan dipasangkan dengan RAM 4 GB. Combines with a large amount of RAM and modern processor, Note 5 could run various demanding applications smoothly, including games and multitasking.

Kamera

Note 5 sports a remarkable camera setup consisting of a 16 MP primary camera with f/1.9 aperture and OIS(Optical Image Stabilization), as well as a 5 MP front-facing camera. Both cameras are capable of capturing high-quality photos, especially in low-light environments. Additionally, the Note 5 also features features such as HDR(High Dynamic Range), slow-motion video recording, and more.

Baterai dan Charging

The Note 5 is powered by a non-removable 3000 mAh battery. Samsung also included support for fast charging, allowing users to top up their battery from 0 to 100% in just 2 hours. This feature was quite impressive for its time.

Layar dan Desain

The Note 5 features a stunning 5.7-inch Super AMOLED display with a resolution of 1440 x 2560 pixels. It also has a sleek and premium design, with a metal frame and Gorilla Glass 4 protection.

Storage dan OS

The Note 5 comes with internal storage options of 32 GB, 64 GB, and 128 GB, expandable up to 128 GB via microSD card. It also runs on Android 5.1.1 Lollipop out of the box, with Samsung's TouchWiz UI on top.

Analisis Harga

The original price of the Samsung Galaxy Note 5 was around$800-$1000, depending on the region and storage capacity. However, prices have varied over time and may depend on the condition, storage capacity, and other factors.

Harga Perluasan

For an examination of the price of the Note 5 during its lifespan, we observed that:

  • New and unused Note 5 devices in their original condition typically started at around $500-$600.
  • Refurbished or used Note 5 devices, even those with minor scratches, could be found for around $300-$500.
  • Salvage or broken Note 5 devices with significant damage could be purchased at a much lower price, often around $200-$400.

Pengalaman Pengguna

Based on reviews and feedback from users at the time, the Note 5 generally received positive feedback for its powerful performance, impressive display, and innovative S-Pen stylus. Some users praised its ability to handle demanding applications and multitasking, while others appreciated its battery life and charging speed.

However, some users experienced issues with the S-Pen, reporting occasional lag or false cursor movement. Others noted that the phone's storage capacity was limited, particularly in the base model.

In conclusion, the Samsung Galaxy Note 5 is a powerful smartphone with impressive specs and features. Despite some drawbacks, it remains a popular choice among fans of the Note series. While prices may vary depending on the condition and storage capacity, the Note 5 offers a great balance of performance, design, and value.
