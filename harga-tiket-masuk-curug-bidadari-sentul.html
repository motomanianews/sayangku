Curug Bidadari Sentul is a popular tourist destination in Indonesia, known for its stunning natural beauty and breathtaking waterfalls. Ticket Prices: What You Need to Know

The ticket prices for Curug Biadari Sentul vary depending on the time of day and season. Here is a breakdown of the ticket prices:

  • Adult ticket: IDR 15,000 per person
  • Child ticket (ages 3-12): IDR 10,000 per person
  • Entrance ticket for foreigners: IDR 25,000 per person

It is recommended to purchase tickets online in advance to avoid long queues on the day of your visit.

Traveling to Curug Bidadari Sentul: Tips and Tricks

Curug Bidadari Sentul is located in the Sentul area of Bogor, West Java. Here are some tips and tricks for traveling to this destination:

  • By Car: The distance from Jakarta to Curug Bidadari Sentul is approximately 60 km, which takes around 1.5 hours to travel by car. You can take the toll road or the regular road, depending on your preference.
  • By Public Transportation: You can take the Trans Bogor bus from Jakarta to Sentul, which takes around 2 hours. From Sentul, you can take a taxi or an ojek (motorcycle taxi) to Curug Bidadari Sentul.
  • By Bike: If you prefer a more eco-friendly option, you can take your bike from Jakarta to Curug Bidadari Sentul. The journey takes around 3 hours and passes through scenic neighborhoods and countryside.

Recommended Activities

Curug Bidadari Sentul offers a range of activities for visitors to enjoy, including:

  • Swimming: The waterfalls at Curug Bidadari Sentul are safe for swimming, making it a great place to cool off during the hot summer months.
  • Trekking: The surrounding area of Curug Bidadari Sentul is lush and green, making it a great place for hiking and trekking.
  • Picnicking: The scenic views and tranquil atmosphere of Curug Bidadari Sentul make it an ideal spot for a picnic with family and friends.
  • Photography: The unique and stunning natural scenery of Curug Bidadari Sentul makes it a great place for photographers to capture some amazing shots.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Curug Bidadari Sentul is a must-visit destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers. With its stunning natural beauty, breathtaking waterfalls, and variety of activities, this destination is sure to leave you with lifelong memories. Remember to plan your trip carefully, including purchasing tickets online in advance and choosing the best transportation option for your needs.
