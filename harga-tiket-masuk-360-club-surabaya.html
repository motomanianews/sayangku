Harga Tiket Masuk 360 Club Surabaya

The entrance fee to the 360 Club Surabaya varies depending on the day of the week and the type of promoter. Here are the current prices to expect:

Weekdays (Monday to Thursday)

  • Entrance fee: IDR 150,000 - IDR 250,000
  • Drink package: IDR 500,000 - IDR 1,000,000
  • VIP table: IDR 2,000,000 - IDR 5,000,000

Weekends (Friday to Sunday)

  • Entrance fee: IDR 200,000 - IDR 350,000
  • Drink package: IDR 800,000 - IDR 1,500,000
  • VIP table: IDR 3,000,000 - IDR 7,000,000

Special Promotions and Events

  • Some days, the 360 Club Surabaya offers special promotions or events that may charge higher entrance fees or offer special packages. For example, during holidays or special occasions, the entrance fee may be higher, or the club may offer special packages that include food and drinks.

What to Expect at 360 Club Surabaya

So, what can you expect when you visit the 360 Club Surabaya? Here are some things that you can look forward to:

Unique Design

  • The 360 Club Surabaya is known for its unique design, which is inspired by ancient temples. The club has a spacious dance floor, and the DJ booth is designed to look like a miniature temple.

Great Music

  • The 360 Club Surabaya features a wide range of genres, from pop and rock to hip-hop and R&B. The club has a resident DJ and many guest DJs, so you can expect to dance the night away.

Food and Drinks

  • The 360 Club Surabaya has a food and drink menu that offers a variety of options, including international dishes and cocktails. You can expect to find a range of food and drink options to suit your tastes.

VIP Area

  • The 360 Club Surabaya has a VIP area that offers a more exclusive experience. The VIP area includes private tables, bottle service, and personalized service.

Tips and Recommendations

Here are some tips and recommendations for visiting the 360 Club Surabaya:

Get There Early

  • If you want to avoid the crowds and get a good spot on the dance floor, it's a good idea to get to the club early.

Dress Code

  • The 360 Club Surabaya has a dress code that is smart casual. Men are expected to wear collared shirts and trousers, while women are expected to wear dresses or skirts.

Respect the Crowd

  • Remember to respect the crowd and fellow patrons. The club can get busy, especially on weekends, so be mindful of your surroundings and keep the noise down.

Enjoy Yourself

  • Most importantly, enjoy yourself! The 360 Club Surabaya is a great place to let loose and have a good time, so be sure to relax and have fun.

Conclusion

The 360 Club Surabaya is a popular nightclub in Surabaya that offers a unique experience.
