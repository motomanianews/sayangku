  3. Consider Junior/Student Discounts: If you're a student or junior, take advantage of the discounted tickets offered for these age groups.

  5. Look for Bundle Deals: CineMaxx Cibubur Junction occasionally offers bundle deals, including ticket and food combos or movie-themed merchandise.
