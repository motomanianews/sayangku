Malioboro Garden Hotel Harga

Admin December 20, 2024

Malioboro Garden Hotel Harga - Malioboro Garden Hotel, a stunning boutique hotel nestled in the heart of Yogyakarta, has become a favorite among travelers seeking a unique and unforgettable experience. With its extravagant architecture, serene surroundings, and exceptional service, this hotel has set the bar high for its competitors. In this article, we'll delve into the world of Malioboro Garden Hotel, exploring its features, amenities, and pricing to help you make an informed decision.

Location and Accessibility

Malioboro Garden Hotel is strategically positioned in the vibrant city of Yogyakarta, within walking distance from the famous Malioboro Street, a bustling shopping and dining destination. The hotel is also close to various historical landmarks, such as the Sultan's Palace, the Water Castle, and the magnificent Borobudur Temple. Guests can easily explore the city's attractions, and then retreat to the peaceful oasis of the hotel.

Getting There

The hotel is approximately 2 kilometers from Adisucipto International Airport, and guests can take a taxi, ride-hailing service, or public transportation to get there. For those arriving by car, parking is available on-site, making it convenient for drivers.

Hotel Features and Amenities

Malioboro Garden Hotel boasts 130 rooms and suites, each thoughtfully designed to provide a comfortable and relaxing stay. The rooms are equipped with modern amenities, including:

  • Air-conditioning
  • Flat-screen TV with international channels
  • Free Wi-Fi
  • Minibar
  • In-room safe
  • Hairdryer
  • Shower with hot and cold water

Guests can unwind in the hotel's outdoor swimming pool, surrounded by lush gardens and tranquil water features. The hotel also offers a fitness center, where you can maintain your fitness regimen. For a more leisurely experience, the hotel has a designated relaxation area with comfortable seating and calming ambiance.

Dining Options

Malioboro Garden Hotel proudly presents its signature restaurant, Garden Bistro, which serves a delightful range of international cuisine, from fusion dishes to traditional Indonesian fare. For a more personalized experience, the hotel offers room service, available from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM.

Room Types and Pricing

Malioboro Garden Hotel offers a range of rooms and suites, each tailored to meet the needs of its discerning guests. Here's a breakdown of their room types and pricing:

| Room Type| Description| Price Range||---|---|---|| Superior Room| Single bed, 30 sqm, city view| IDR 1,200,000- IDR 1,500,000 per night|| Deluxe Room| Double bed, 40 sqm, city view| IDR 1,500,000- IDR 1,800,000 per night|| Junior Suite| Large double bed, 60 sqm, garden view| IDR 2,500,000- IDR 3,000,000 per night|| Suite| Separate living room, king-sized bed, 80 sqm, garden view| IDR 4,000,000- IDR 5,000,000 per night|

Please note that prices are subject to change and may vary depending on the season and availability.

Conclusion

Malioboro Garden Hotel is an oasis of tranquility in the heart of Yogyakarta, offering a unique blend of comfort, relaxation, and convenience. With its exceptional service, beautiful architecture, and stunning surroundings, this hotel is an ideal choice for those seeking a memorable experience in this enchanting city.
