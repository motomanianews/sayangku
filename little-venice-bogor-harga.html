Little Venice Bogor Harga

Admin December 20, 2024

Little Venice Bogor Harga - Little Venice Bogor, a picturesque tourist destinationlocated in Bogor, West Java, Indonesia, is a must-visit place for anyone seeking serenity and natural beauty. This charming little town is built on a 12-acre plot of land surrounding a serene lake, showcasing unique Dutch-inspired architecture and a variety of flora and fauna. In this article, we will explore the wonders of Little Venice Bogor and provide an update on its current pricing.

History and Concept

Little Venice Bogor was founded in 2000 by entrepreneurs who sought to create a unique tourist destination in Indonesia. Inspired by the ancient city of Venice in Italy, the brainchild of Little Venice Bogor aimed to provide a tranquil environment for visitors to relax and unwind. The creators drew inspiration from the city's canals, bridges, and ornate architecture to build a replica of this iconic city in the midst of lush greenery.

Places to Visit

  1. Lake and Surroundings: The central attraction of Little Venice Bogor is the serene lake, surrounded by gardens, waterfalls, and bridges. Visitors can take leisurely boat rides or simply sit and relax amidst the peaceful atmosphere.
  2. Museum: The on-site museum showcases the history and development of Little Venice Bogor, offering insights into the town's unique concept and evolution.
  3. Bridges and Canals: Explore the charming bridges and canals, reminiscent of Venice, while taking in the picturesque views of the surrounding landscape.
  4. Garden and Park: Wander through the lush greenery, admiring the beautiful flora and fauna, and spot some of the animals that inhabit the area.
  5. Venetian-style Buildings: Marvel at the intricately designed Venetian-inspired buildings, which add to the town's charm and authenticity.

Activities

  1. Boat Ride: Take a leisurely boat ride around the lake, enjoying the tranquility and natural beauty of the surroundings.
  2. Walking Tours: Join guided walking tours to explore the town's hidden gems and learn about its history and concept.
  3. Food and Beverage: Indulge in local and international cuisine at one of the on-site restaurants or cafes, offering a range of mouth-watering dishes and refreshing drinks.
  4. Shopping: Browse the various souvenir shops and stalls, selling unique local products, mementos, and handicrafts.

Entrance Fee and Prices

The entrance fee to Little Venice Bogor varies depending on the package and activities chosen:

  • Standard Package: IDR 110,000 (approximately USD 8) per person, which includes access to the park and lake.
  • Premium Package: IDR 250,000 (approximately USD 18) per person, which includes access to all areas, including the museum and guided tours.
  • Food and Beverage Packages: IDR 100,000 to IDR 500,000 (approximately USD 7 to USD 35) per person, depending on the type of meal and drink.

Conclusion

Little Venice Bogor is a must-visit destination for anyone seeking a relaxing and unique experience. With its serene lake, charming architecture, and lush greenery, this town is a true paradise on earth. Whether you're looking for a romantic getaway, a family vacation, or a solo retreat, Little Venice Bogor has something for everyone. Plan your visit today and experience the wonders of this little slice of heaven!
