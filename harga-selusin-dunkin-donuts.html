Harga Selusin Dunkin Donuts

Admin November 8, 2024

Harga Selusin Dunkin Donuts - Dunkin' Donuts is a popular global coffee and baked goods chain that has gained a massive following among coffee lovers. Known for its wide range of mouth-watering donuts, sandwiches, and coffee drinks, Dunkin' Donuts has become a household name. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to decide which one to choose. In this article, we'll explore the varied prices of Dunkin' Donuts and help you find the best value for your money.

What's the Average Price of Dunkin' Donuts?

The average price range of Dunkin' Donuts varies depending on the location, size, and type of item. On average, a single donut can cost between IDR 15,000 to IDR 25,000(approximately USD 1 to USD 2). A hot coffee drink typically falls in the range of IDR 15,000 to IDR 25,000 as well. Breakfast sandwiches, on the other hand, can range from IDR 30,000 to IDR 50,000(approximately USD 2 to USD 4).

Which Dunkin' Donuts Items Offer the Best Value?

Some Dunkin' Donuts items stand out as particularly good value for money. Here are a few options to consider:

  • Coffee and Donut Combo: For around IDR 30,000 to IDR 40,000 (approximately USD 2 to USD 3), you can get a coffee and a donut, which is a great way to satisfy your caffeine and sweet cravings.
  • Box of Donuts: If you're planning a snack or brunch with friends, consider buying a box of donuts, which usually costs between IDR 50,000 to IDR 100,000 (approximately USD 4 to USD 8). You can get 6 to 12 donuts depending on the size and type.
  • Flatbread Sandwiches: For around IDR 50,000 to IDR 70,000 (approximately USD 4 to USD 5), you can get a flavorful flatbread sandwich, which is a great option for a quick and satisfying meal.

Tips for Saving Money at Dunkin' Donuts

Here are some tips to help you save money at Dunkin' Donuts:

  • Order a smaller size: Instead of getting a large coffee or donut, opt for a smaller size to save money.
  • Choose the right time: Visit Dunkin' Donuts during off-peak hours or during promotions to get discounts or special deals.
  • Use coupons and vouchers: Take advantage of online coupons, mobile apps, and promotions to get discounts and free items.
  • Consider a DIY breakfast: Instead of buying a breakfast sandwich, create your own with a bagel or English muffin and add some cream cheese and jam for a fraction of the cost.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Dunkin' Donuts offers a range of prices for its delicious donuts, sandwiches, and coffee drinks. By knowing what to order and how to save money, you can enjoy your favorite treats without breaking the bank. Whether you're in the mood for a classic donut, a savory sandwich, or a hot cup of coffee, Dunkin' Donuts has something for everyone. With its convenient locations, flexible pricing, and rewarding loyalty program, Dunkin' Donuts is the perfect spot to start your day or grab a quick snack.
