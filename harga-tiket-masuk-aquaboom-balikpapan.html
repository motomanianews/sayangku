Harga Tiket Masuk Aquaboom Balikpapan

Admin November 28, 2024

Harga Tiket Masuk Aquaboom Balikpapan - Are you planning a trip to Balikpapan and looking for information on the ticket prices to Aquaboom Balikpapan? You're in the right place! In this article, we'll provide you with a comprehensive guide on the ticket prices, opening hours, and everything you need to know about Aquaboom Balikpapan.

Introduction

Aquaboom Balikpapan is a popular water park located in Balikpapan, East Kalimantan, Indonesia. The park offers various water slides, wave pools, and other attractions that cater to visitors of all ages. In this article, we'll focus on the ticket prices and provide you with a step-by-step guide on how to plan your visit.

Ticket Prices

The ticket prices for Aquaboom Balikpapan vary depending on the type of ticket and the day of the week. Here are the current ticket prices:

Normal Ticket

  • Adult: IDR 150,000
  • Children (above 120 cm): IDR 120,000
  • Children (below 120 cm): IDR 80,000

Peak Season Ticket

  • Adult: IDR 200,000
  • Children (above 120 cm): IDR 160,000
  • Children (below 120 cm): IDR 120,000

Off-Peak Season Ticket

  • Adult: IDR 120,000
  • Children (above 120 cm): IDR 100,000
  • Children (below 120 cm): IDR 80,000

Family Ticket

  • 2 Adults and 2 Children (above 120 cm): IDR 450,000
  • 2 Adults and 2 Children (below 120 cm): IDR 360,000

Online Ticket

  • Adult: IDR 130,000 (includes online discount)
  • Children (above 120 cm): IDR 110,000 (includes online discount)
  • Children (below 120 cm): IDR 90,000 (includes online discount)

Opening Hours

Aquaboom Balikpapan is open daily from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. However, the park may have special events or themed days that may affect the opening hours. It's always best to check the park's website or social media page for the latest information.

How to Plan Your Visit

Planning your visit to Aquaboom Balikpapan is easy! Here are some tips to help you make the most of your day:

Arrival and Parking

  • The park is located at Jalan Raya Panginungan, Grogol, Balikpapan.
  • Parking is available on site, with a limited number of parking spaces.
  • Visitors are advised to arrive at least 30 minutes before the park's opening hours to avoid congestion.

Changing and Shower Facilities

  • The park provides free changing and shower facilities for visitors.
  • Visitors are advised to use the shower facilities before entering the water park to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience.

Food and Beverage

  • The park offers a variety of food and beverage options, including restaurants, cafes, and snack bars.
  • Visitors are advised to bring their own food and drinks to avoid queuing for the park's facilities.

Safety and Emergency

  • The park takes the safety and security of its visitors seriously.
  • Visitors are advised to follow the park's rules and regulations, including the dress code and the prohibited items.
  • In case of an emergency, visitors can contact the park's staff or the security team.

Conclusion

In conclusion, Aquaboom Balikpapan is a great place to visit for families and friends who love water parks. With its affordable ticket prices, spacious facilities, and fun attractions, it's no wonder why it's a popular destination in Balikpapan. By following the tips and guidelines outlined in this article, you'll be able to plan your visit and make the most of your time at the park. Happy swimming!
