  • Memori Internal: 64GB
  • Kamera: 13MP/5MP
  • Layar: 5.5 inci, HD (1280x720) IPS
  • Baterai: 3000mAh, non-removable
  • Sim Card: Dual SIM (Nano-SIM/Nano-SIM)
  • Jaringan: 4G, 3G, 2G
