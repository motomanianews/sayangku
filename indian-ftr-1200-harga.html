Indian Ftr 1200 Harga - Indian FTR 1200: Harga dan Spesifikasi Terkini

The Indian FTR 1200 is a highly anticipated motorcycle that has been generating a lot of buzz in the motorcycle community. As the first production bike to be designed by Indian Motorcycle's Product Development team in collaboration with Greg many, a legendary motorcycle designer, the FTR 1200 is a game-changer. In this article, we will dive into the details of the Indian FTR 1200, including its price, specifications, and features.

Harga Indian FTR 1200

The Indian FTR 1200 is priced at around IDR 250 million(approximately USD 18,000) for the base trim, while the top-of-the-line model with optional accessories and upgrades can cost upwards of IDR 350 million(approximately USD 24,000).

Spesifikasi Indian FTR 1200

The Indian FTR 1200 is powered by a liquid-cooled 1,169cc V-twin engine that produces 120 horsepower and 90 ft-lbs of torque. The bike features a six-speed manual transmission and a slip-assist clutch. The FTR 1200 has a seat height of 31.5 inches and a wet weight of 460 pounds.

Fitur Indian FTR 1200

The Indian FTR 1200 comes with a range of features that make it a standout in its class. Some of the notable features include:

  • Design: The FTR 1200 has a sleek and aggressive design inspired by the World Superbike racing machines. The bike features a bold and purposeful look with a low seat height and a passenger seat.
  • Suspension: The FTR 1200 has a 43mm USD fork up front and a monoshock rear suspension, providing a smooth ride and excellent handling.
  • Brakes: The bike features dual 320mm discs with radially-mounted Brembo calipers and a 260mm disc with a floating Brembo caliper at the rear.
  • Instrument Cluster: The FTR 1200 has a high-resolution 6.5-inch touchscreen instrument cluster that provides a range of information, including speed, ODO, and navigation.
  • Ride-by-Wire: The bike features a ride-by-wire system that provides a more responsive and intuitive throttle response.

Variasi Indian FTR 1200

The Indian FTR 1200 is available in three different styles: the standard model, the Low Seat model, and the Carbon Fiber model. The Low Seat model features a lower seat height and a lower footpeg height, making it ideal for shorter riders. The Carbon Fiber model features a carbon fiber bodywork and a range of carbon fiber accessories.

Kesimpulan

The Indian FTR 1200 is a highly capable and feature-rich motorcycle that offers a unique blend of performance, handling, and design. With its competitive price, the FTR 1200 is an excellent option for riders who are looking for a bike that can tackle both the streets and the track. Whether you're a seasoned rider or a newcomer to the world of motorcycles, the Indian FTR 1200 is definitely worth considering.
