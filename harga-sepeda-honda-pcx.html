Harga Sepeda Honda Pcx - ADC lahGrade since the introduction of the Honda PCX in the late 2000s, this scooter has become a popular choice among commuters and riders alike. With its compact design, fuel efficiency, and reliability, it's no wonder why many people opt for this stylish and practical ride.

In this article, we'll delve into the world of Honda PCX and explore its features, pros and cons, and most importantly, the current market price of this impressive scooter.

Spesifikasi Honda PCX

Before diving into the price, let's take a look at the specifications of the Honda PCX. This scooter is equipped with a 244cc single-cylinder engine, producing 18.5 horsepower and 19.5 Nm of torque. Features include:

  • Max power: 18.5 kW @ 6,500 rpm
  • Max torque: 19.5 Nm @ 5,250 rpm
  • Fuel capacity: 8.5 liters
  • Fuel efficiency: Up to 43 km/L
  • Brakes: Combined braking system with ABS

Jenis dan Variasi Honda PCX

The Honda PCX comes in various models, each with its unique features and price points. Here are some of the most popular variants:

  • PCX 150: The base model, which offers a more affordable option for commuters.
  • PCX 180: The mid-range model, which adds more features such as ABS and a more powerful engine.
  • PCX 180 ABS: The top-of-the-line model, which comes with advanced features like ABS and LED headlights.

Harga Honda PCX di Indonesia

So, how much does it cost to own a Honda PCX in Indonesia? Here are the current market prices for each variant:

  • PCX 150: IDR 14.5 million - IDR 17.5 million
  • PCX 180: IDR 20.5 million - IDR 24.5 million
  • PCX 180 ABS: IDR 25.5 million - IDR 30.5 million

Please note that prices may vary depending on the dealership, location, and any additional features or accessories.

Tips Memilih Harga Sepeda Honda PCX yang Tepat

When choosing the right price for your Honda PCX, consider the following tips:

  • Research and compare prices from different dealerships and online marketplaces
  • Consider the condition and mileage of the scooter
  • Check if the scooter is still under warranty
  • Negotiate the price, especially if you're purchasing from a private seller

Benefit dan Drawback Honda PCX

Here are some of the benefits and drawbacks of owning a Honda PCX:

Benefit:

  • Fuel efficiency: Up to 43 km/L
  • Compact design: Easy to maneuver in tight spaces
  • Reliable engine: Fewer maintenance costs
  • Affordable: Prices range from IDR 14.5 million to IDR 30.5 million

Drawback:

  • Limited storage space: Not ideal for long trips or commuting with large gear
  • No windshield: Can be uncomfortable for taller riders
  • Not as powerful as some other scooters: 18.5 horsepower may not be enough for some riders

Kesimpulan

The Honda PCX is a reliable, fuel-efficient, and affordable scooter that's perfect for commuters and casual riders. With its compact design and advanced features, it's no wonder why many people opt for this stylish and practical ride. In conclusion, the current market price of the Honda PCX ranges from IDR 14.5 million to IDR 30.5 million, depending on the variant and condition. By considering the tips and factors mentioned above, you'll be able to find the right price for your Honda PCX.
