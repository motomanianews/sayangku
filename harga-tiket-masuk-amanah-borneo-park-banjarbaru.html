Harga Tiket Masuk Amanah Borneo Park Banjarbaru

Admin November 28, 2024

Harga Tiket Masuk Amanah Borneo Park Banjarbaru - Located in the heart of Banjarbaru, South Kalimantan, Amanah Borneo Park is a must-visit destination for nature enthusiasts. The park offers a tranquil escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, with its lush greenery, winding rivers, and scenic views. In this article, we will explore the prices of entrance tickets to Amanah Borneo Park, what you can expect to see and experience during your visit, and why it's a popular spot among locals and tourists alike.

Admission Fees

The admission fees to Amanah Borneo Park vary depending on the visitor's age and nationality. Here are the current prices:

Indonesian Citizens

  • Adult: IDR 15,000 (approximately USD 1.06)
  • Child (6-12 years old): IDR 10,000 (approximately USD 0.71)
  • Child (under 6 years old): Free

Foreign Visitors

  • Adult: IDR 30,000 (approximately USD 2.12)
  • Child (6-12 years old): IDR 20,000 (approximately USD 1.42)
  • Child (under 6 years old): Free

Activities and Attractions

Once you've purchased your entrance ticket, you can explore the various attractions and activities offered by Amanah Borneo Park. Here are some of the things you can expect to see and do:

Waterfalls and Rivers

Amanah Borneo Park is home to several waterfalls and rivers, perfect for swimming, fishing, or simply enjoying the scenic views. The park's staff are also happy to provide kayaks and paddleboats for rent.

Hiking Trails

For the more adventurous visitors, the park offers several hiking trails that wind through the lush jungle and offer stunning views of the surrounding landscape.

Cultural Village

The park's cultural village is home to several traditional houses and structures, showcasing the local culture and heritage of Banjarbaru.

Food and Beverages

There are several food and beverage stalls throughout the park, offering a range of local and international cuisine. From traditional Indonesian dishes to fresh fruit and snacks, there's something to suit every taste.

Picnic Areas

Amanah Borneo Park has several picnic areas, perfect for a relaxing meal or snack. You can bring your own food and enjoy the peaceful surroundings, or purchase food and drinks from the park's vendors.

Why Visit Amanah Borneo Park?

So, why should you visit Amanah Borneo Park? Here are just a few reasons:

Unspoiled Nature

Amanah Borneo Park offers a unique opportunity to experience the unspoiled nature of South Kalimantan. With its lush forests, winding rivers, and scenic waterfalls, the park is a haven for nature lovers and outdoor enthusiasts.

Cultural Significance

The park's cultural village offers a fascinating glimpse into the local culture and heritage of Banjarbaru. You can learn about the history and traditions of the region, and even participate in traditional activities and crafts.

Relaxation and Stress Relief

Amanah Borneo Park is the perfect place to relax and unwind. With its peaceful surroundings, scenic views, and tranquil atmosphere, the park is a great place to escape the stresses of daily life.

Family-Friendly

Amanah Borneo Park offers something for everyone, from hiking trails and picnic areas to waterfalls and cultural exhibits. The park is a great place for families to spend a day together, enjoying the beautiful scenery and each other's company.

In conclusion, Amanah Borneo Park is a must-visit destination for nature lovers, outdoor enthusiasts, and anyone looking to experience the unique culture and heritage of Banjarbaru. With its affordable admission fees, range of activities and attractions, and stunning natural beauty, the park is a treasure trove of experiences waiting to be discovered. So why not visit Amanah Borneo Park today and experience it for yourself?
