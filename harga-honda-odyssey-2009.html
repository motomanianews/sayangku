Harga Honda Odyssey 2009

Admin November 1, 2024

Harga Honda Odyssey 2009 - The Honda Odyssey is a renowned minivan that has been a favorite among families and caregivers for years. First introduced in 1994, this versatile vehicle has undergone significant transformations to cater to the changing needs of its customers. In this article, we will delve into the 2009 model of the Honda Odyssey, exploring its features, specifications, and value for money.

Overview

The 2009 Honda Odyssey is the fourth generation of this popular minivan. With its sleek and modern design, this vehicle boasts a spacious cabin, plenty of storage space, and innovative features that make it an ideal choice for families, caregivers, and individuals who need to transport multiple passengers.

Spesifikasi

Some of the key features of the 2009 Honda Odyssey include:

  • Engine: 3.5-liter V6 DOHC 24-valve i-VTEC engine producing 255 horsepower and 250 lb-ft of torque
  • Transmission: 5-speed automatic transmission with SportShift manual-mode transmission
  • Drive Type: Front-wheel drive
  • Fuel Economy: 20 miles per gallon (mpg) in the city, 28 mpg on the highway, and 23 mpg combined
  • Seating Capacity: 7 passengers (standard configuration)
  • Cargo Space: 38.4 cubic feet behind the third row, 91.0 cubic feet behind the second row, and 168.5 cubic feet with all seats folded
  • Dimensions: 203.9 inches long, 78.5 inches wide, and 68.5 inches tall

Harga

The 2009 Honda Odyssey is no longer in production, and as such, the prices for this model may vary depending on the trim level, condition, and mileage. Based on market research, here are some approximate price ranges for the 2009 Honda Odyssey:

  • EX-L trim: IDR 250-350 million (approximately USD 18,000-25,000)
  • EX-L with navigation trim: IDR 330-450 million (approximately USD 23,000-32,000)
  • Touring trim: IDR 450-600 million (approximately USD 32,000-43,000)

Review

The 2009 Honda Odyssey has received favorable reviews from critics and owners alike. Here are some pros and cons to consider:

Pros:

  • Spacious cabin with ample legroom and headroom for passengers
  • Innovative feature such as the " Magic Seat" which allows for multiple seating configurations
  • Smooth and quiet ride due to its advanced noise-reduction technology
  • Impressive fuel economy for its class

Cons:

  • Some users have reported issues with the 5-speed automatic transmission
  • The cabin can feel a bit noisy at high speeds
  • Some critics have noted that the exterior design is less aggressive than previous models

Konten terkini

The Honda Odyssey has undergone significant updates since the 2009 model year. In 2011, the Odyssey received a major facelift, featuring a new exterior design, updated safety features, and improved fuel efficiency. In 2018, Honda introduced the Odyssey's fifth generation, which boasts an even more modern design, advanced technology features, and a more powerful engine.

Conclusion

The 2009 Honda Odyssey is a reliable and feature-rich minivan that offers excellent value for its price. While it may not have all the bells and whistles of newer models, its spacious cabin, innovative features, and impressive fuel economy make it an attractive option for families and caregivers. Additionally, the Odyssey's strong resale value and low maintenance costs make it a smart investment for long-term owners.

Disclaimer

The prices and specifications mentioned in this article are approximate and may vary depending on the trim level, condition, and mileage of the vehicle.
