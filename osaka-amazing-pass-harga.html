What is the Osaka Amazing Pass?

The Osaka Amazing Pass is a 2-day or 3-day sightseeing pass that grants unlimited access to 28 popular destinations in Osaka, including museums, gardens, and theme parks. The pass is designed to help travelers save both time and money by allowing them to visit multiple attractions without the hassle of buying individual tickets.

How Does the Pass Work?

To purchase the Osaka Amazing Pass, simply visit the official website and choose your preferred pass duration(2-day or 3-day). Once you receive your pass, you can start using it to visit the participating attractions listed below.

Pricing and Availability
