Harga Tiket Masuk Selecta Terbaru - Selecta, destinations that are often crowded with visitors, not only locals but also tourists. This park has a unique concept, providing a wide range of recreational activities for visitors of all ages. In this article, we will discuss the latest ticket prices for Selecta, tips for visiting, and some recommendations to make your trip more enjoyable.

Sejarah Selecta

Selecta is a new theme park located in the city of Yogyakarta, Java, Indonesia. Opened in 2014, this park has been gaining popularity among locals and tourists alike. The park is designed to offer an exciting experience for visitors, with its varied range of recreational activities, such as thrill rides, water rides, and many more.

Harga Tiket Masuk Selecta Terbaru

As previously mentioned, the ticket prices for Selecta have changed over time. The latest prices are as follows:

Adult Ticket (11-59 years old)

  • One-day pass: IDR 250,000 (approximately USD 18)
  • Two-day pass: IDR 450,000 (approximately USD 32)
  • Birthday pass: IDR 350,000 (approximately USD 25)

Child Ticket (4-10 years old)

  • One-day pass: IDR 200,000 (approximately USD 14)
  • Two-day pass: IDR 350,000 (approximately USD 25)
  • Birthday pass: IDR 250,000 (approximately USD 18)

Senior Citizen Ticket (60 years old and above)

  • One-day pass: IDR 150,000 (approximately USD 11)
  • Two-day pass: IDR 250,000 (approximately USD 18)
  • Birthday pass: IDR 200,000 (approximately USD 14)

Tips untuk Tiba di Selecta

To ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience at Selecta, here are some tips:

Plan Your Visit

Plan your visit in advance to avoid crowds and long queues. Choose the best time to visit, when the weather is pleasant, and the park is less crowded.

Wear Comfortable Shoes

The park is quite large, so it's essential to wear comfortable shoes to ensure your feet don't get tired from walking.

Bring Sun Protection

Don't forget to bring sun protection, such as sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat to protect yourself from the sun.

Follow Park Rules

Follow the park rules and guidelines to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for yourself and other visitors.

Pengalaman di Selecta

Visitors can experience a wide range of recreational activities at Selecta, including:

Thrill Rides

The park features several thrill rides, such as roller coasters, water slides, and drop tower rides.

Water Rides

There are many water rides to choose from, including wave pools, lazy rivers, and splash pads for kids.

Live Shows

Selecta offers live shows, such as dance performances, musical performances, and comedy shows.

Rekomendasi untuk Pengalaman yang Lebih Baik

To make your trip to Selecta even more enjoyable, here are some recommendations:

Arrive Early

Arrive early to beat the crowds and enjoy the park's attractions with less wait.

Try the Food and Beverages

Sample the park's food and beverages, including snacks and meals at the various restaurants and food stalls.

Take Breaks

Take breaks during the day to rest and recharge, especially if you're visiting with children.

Plan for a Picnic

If you want to enjoy the park's scenery, plan for a picnic and bring your own food.

In conclusion, Selecta is an exciting theme park that offers a unique experience for visitors of all ages. By following the latest ticket prices, tips, and recommendations, you can ensure a smooth and enjoyable experience.
