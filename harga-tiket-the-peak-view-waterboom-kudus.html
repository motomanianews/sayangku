Introduction

Harga Tiket The Peak View Waterboom Kudus -

Harga Tiket The Peak View Waterboom Kudus - Located in Kudus, Central Java, Indonesia, The Peak View Waterboom is a popular destination for thrill-seekers and families alike. This water park offers an exciting experience with its unique attractions, slides, and pools, making it a must-visit for anyone looking to beat the heat and have a blast. But before you plan your trip, one question often comes to mind: what's the price of a ticket to The Peak View Waterboom?

Ticket Prices

The ticket prices for The Peak View Waterboom vary depending on the day and packages you choose. Here's a breakdown of the current prices:

  • Weekdays (Monday to Friday):
    • Adult (12 years and above): IDR 350,000 (approximately USD 24)
    • Child (6-11 years old): IDR 270,000 (approximately USD 19)
    • Toddler (3-5 years old): IDR 220,000 (approximately USD 15)
  • Weekends (Saturday and Sunday):
    • Adult (12 years and above): IDR 450,000 (approximately USD 31)
    • Child (6-11 years old): IDR 350,000 (approximately USD 24)
    • Toddler (3-5 years old): IDR 280,000 (approximately USD 20)
  • Special Packages:
    • Couple Package (2 adults): IDR 700,000 (approximately USD 49)
    • Family Package (2 adults + 2 children): IDR 1,400,000 (approximately USD 98)

Please note that prices may be subject to change, so it's always best to check the official website or contact the water park directly for the latest information.

What to Expect

The Peak View Waterboom is designed to provide an unforgettable experience for visitors of all ages. The park features a variety of attractions, including:

  • Water Slides: Enjoy thrilling rides with 10 meters of vertical drop, winding slides, and even a giant dump bucket that soaks the area below.
  • Wave Pool: Experience the rush of riding giant waves in a pool designed for all ages.
  • Lazy River: Take a relaxing float down a winding river that passes through lush vegetation and water features.
  • Kids' Play Area: Let the little ones loose in a dedicated area filled with interactive water toys and splash pads.
  • Water Play: Cool off and have fun in the sun with water guns, water cannons, and a massive water splash pad.

Tips and Tricks

Before you head to The Peak View Waterboom, here are a few tips to keep in mind:

  • Arrive Early: Beat the crowds and get the most out of your day by arriving at the park early.
  • Wear Comfortable Clothing: Dress in comfortable, quick-drying clothing and footwear to ensure a fun and safe experience.
  • Bring Sunscreen and Hats: Protect yourself from the sun with sunscreen, hats, and sunglasses.
  • Don't Forget Your Swimsuit: You'll want to be comfortable and ready to dive in at a moment's notice!
  • Check the Website: Stay up-to-date with the latest news, promotions, and operating hours by checking the official The Peak View Waterboom website.

Conclusion

The Peak View Waterboom Kudus is an exciting water park experience that's sure to thrill visitors of all ages. With its unique attractions, slides, and pools, it's a must-visit destination for anyone looking to cool off and have a blast. By understanding the ticket prices and what to expect, you can plan your trip and make the most of your day. Don't forget to follow the tips and tricks to ensure a fun and safe experience.
