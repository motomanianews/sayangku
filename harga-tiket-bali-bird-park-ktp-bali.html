Harga Tiket Bali Bird Park Ktp Bali - Harga Tiket Bali Bird Park: Everything You Need to Know

Bali Bird Park is a popular tourist destination in Bali, Indonesia, that attracts millions of visitors every year. The park is home to over 100 species of birds from around the world, including some of the most unique and endangered species. As one of the most visited attractions in Bali, it's essential to know what to expect and how to plan your visit. In this article, we'll cover everything you need to know about the Bali Bird Park, including the ticket prices.

Ticket Prices for Bali Bird Park

The ticket prices for Bali Bird Park vary depending on your age, nationality, and the type of ticket you purchase. Here's a breakdown of the prices:

  • Adults:
    • Local (Indonesian citizens with KTP/ID card):
      • Indonesian citizen with KTP/ID card: IDR 220,000 (approximately USD 15)
      • Non-Indonesian citizen with valid KTP/ID card: IDR 250,000 (approximately USD 18)
    • International visitors:
      • Adult: IDR 390,000 (approximately USD 27)
  • Children:
    • Local (Indonesian citizens with KTP/ID card):
      • Indonesian citizen with KTP/ID card: IDR 140,000 (approximately USD 10)
      • Non-Indonesian citizen with valid KTP/ID card: IDR 170,000 (approximately USD 12)
    • International visitors:
      • Child (4-12 years old): IDR 260,000 (approximately USD 18)
  • Special Offers:
    • Family Package (2 adults + 2 children): IDR 1,140,000 (approximately USD 80)
    • Foreigner Package (includes entrance ticket, transportation, and lunch): IDR 520,000 (approximately USD 37)

What's Included in the Ticket Price

The ticket price includes:

  • Admission to the park
  • Access to the bird shows
  • Entrance to the aviary exhibitions
  • Feeding the birds (limited to certain species)
  • Access to the playground and kid's activities
  • Free parking

Tips and Tricks for Visiting Bali Bird Park

  • Plan your visit: Visit early in the morning to avoid the crowds and heat.
  • Dress appropriately: Wear comfortable clothing and shoes, and bring sunscreen and a hat to protect yourself from the sun.
  • Bring water: Staying hydrated is essential in Bali's tropical climate.
  • Don't miss the shows: The bird shows are a highlight of your visit, so make sure to catch them.
  • Interact with the animals: Many of the birds are able to fly and can be fed, so be sure to interact with them.
  • Get up close: There are several viewing areas where you can get up close to the birds, including the walk-in aviary.

Conclusion

Bali Bird Park is a must-visit destination for any bird enthusiast or family looking for a fun and educational experience. With its vast collection of birds, bird shows, and interactive activities, it's an attraction that has something for everyone. Remember to plan your visit, dress appropriately, and bring water to ensure a comfortable and enjoyable experience.
