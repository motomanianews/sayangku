Oppo F7 Plus Harga Dan Spesifikasi

Admin December 22, 2024

Oppo F7 Plus Harga Dan Spesifikasi - Oppo F7 Plus: Review dan Spesifikasi Handphone yang diincar

As one of the leading smartphone manufacturers in the world, Oppo has been known for its innovative technology and stylish design. The Oppo F7 Plus is one of the most highly anticipated smartphones from the company, and for good reason. In this article, we will take a closer look at the Oppo F7 Plus's specifications, features, and price to help you decide if it's the right phone for you.

Desain dan Tampilan

The Oppo F7 Plus boasts a sleek and stylish design, with a glossy rear panel and a 6.3-inch full HD+ display. The phone's dimensions are 156.5 x 76 x 7.8 mm, making it comfortable to hold and easy to use with one hand. The display is one of the highlights of the phone, with a resolution of 1080 x 2340 pixels and an aspect ratio of 19.5:9.

Spesifikasi Teknis

Under the hood, the Oppo F7 Plus packs a Qualcomm Snapdragon 660 processor, which provides smooth performance and efficient battery life. The phone comes with 4GB or 6GB of RAM, depending on the variant, and 64GB or 128GB of internal storage. The storage can be expanded up to 256GB via a microSD card.

The phone is powered by a large 3400mAh battery, which is non-removable. Oppo has also included fast charging technology, which can fill up the battery to 100% in just 1.5 hours.

Kamera dan Fungsi

The Oppo F7 Plus boasts a dual camera setup, with a 16-megapixel primary sensor and a 2-megapixel secondary sensor. The camera app is easy to use, with features like portrait mode, Bokeh mode, and HDR. The phone can also record 4K videos at 30fps.

The phone also features a 25-megapixel front camera, which is ideal for selfies and video calls.

Fitur Tambahan

In addition to its impressive specifications, the Oppo F7 Plus comes with a range of features that make it a great value for money. Some of the notable features include:

  • Fingerprint sensor: The phone features a fingerprint sensor on the back, which is quick and easy to use.
  • Face unlock: The Oppo F7 Plus also features face unlock, which can be used as an alternative to fingerprint unlock.
  • NFC: The phone supports NFC, which allows for wireless payments and data transfer.
  • USB-C: The phone has a USB-C port, which is faster and more convenient than traditional micro-USB ports.

Harga dan Rilis

The Oppo F7 Plus is available in several countries, including Indonesia, China, and India. The phone is priced starting from around IDR 5.5 million for the base variant with 4GB of RAM and 64GB of storage.

Komparasi dengan lawan

The Oppo F7 Plus has some stiff competition in the market, with phones like the Samsung Galaxy A9, Huawei P20 Pro, and Xiaomi Redmi Note 5 Pro. Here's a brief comparison with some of its main rivals:

| Phone| Display| Processor| RAM| Storage| Camera| Battery||---|---|---|---|---|---|---|| Oppo F7 Plus| 6.3-inch FHD+| Snapdragon 660| 4GB/6GB| 64GB/128GB| Dual 16MP+ 2MP| 3400mAh|| Samsung Galaxy A9| 6.3-inch FHD+| Snapdragon 660| 4GB/6GB| 128GB+ microSD| Quad 24MP+ 8MP+ 10MP+ 5MP| 3800mAh|| Huawei P20 Pro| 6.1-inch FHD+| Kirin 970| 6GB| 128GB+ microSD| Triple 12MP+ 20MP+ 8MP| 3340mAh|| Xiaomi Redmi Note 5 Pro| 5.99-inch FHD+| Snapdragon 636| 4GB/6GB| 64GB/128GB| Dual 12MP+ 5MP| 4000mAh|

As you can see, the Oppo F7 Plus is a solid contender in the market, with impressive specifications and a stylish design. However, its competitors are not to be underestimated, with their own unique features and advantages.

Kesimpulan

The Oppo F7 Plus is a great value for money, with a range of impressive specifications and features. Its large display, dual camera setup, and fast charging technology make it a compelling option for those in the market for a new smartphone. However, it's worth considering the competition before making a decision.
