Harga Tiket Dm Tirta Pandeglang

Admin November 24, 2024

Harga Tiket Dm Tirta Pandeglang - Are you planning a trip to Tirta Pandeglang, West Java, Indonesia? Tirta Pandeglang is a popular tourist destination known for its natural beauty, hot springs, and unique culture. To make your trip more enjoyable, we have compiled a comprehensive guide on the latest pricing for DM Tirta Pandeglang tickets.

What is DM Tirta Pandeglang?

DM Tirta Pandeglang is a German-engineered hot spring resort located in Pandeglang, West Java. The resort offers a range of facilities, including hot springs, swimming pools, water slides, and a restaurant. Visitors can enjoy a relaxing day at the resort, rejuvenating their body and mind amidst the natural beauty of the surroundings.

Harga Tiket DM Tirta Pandeglang

The ticket prices for DM Tirta Pandeglang vary depending on the type of ticket and the number of people visiting. Here are the latest ticket prices for DM Tirta Pandeglang:

Single Ticket

  • Adult ticket: IDR 150,000 - IDR 250,000 (approximately USD 10 - USD 18)
  • Child ticket (ages 3-12): IDR 100,000 - IDR 150,000 (approximately USD 7 - USD 10)

Family Ticket

  • Family ticket (2 adults and 2 children): IDR 500,000 - IDR 750,000 (approximately USD 35 - USD 52)
  • Family ticket (1 adult and 3 children): IDR 350,000 - IDR 550,000 (approximately USD 25 - USD 38)

Group Ticket

  • Group ticket (10-20 people): IDR 500,000 - IDR 1,000,000 (approximately USD 35 - USD 70)
  • Group ticket (21-30 people): IDR 750,000 - IDR 1,500,000 (approximately USD 52 - USD 105)

What's Included in the Ticket Price?

The ticket price for DM Tirta Pandeglang includes access to the resort's facilities, including:

  • Hot springs
  • Swimming pools
  • Water slides
  • Restaurant
  • Parking

Visitors can also rent additional facilities, such as:

  • Lockers: IDR 20,000 - IDR 50,000 (approximately USD 1.4 - USD 3.5) per day
  • towels: IDR 20,000 - IDR 50,000 (approximately USD 1.4 - USD 3.5) per day
  • Umbrellas: IDR 10,000 - IDR 20,000 (approximately USD 0.7 - USD 1.4) per day

Tips for Visiting DM Tirta Pandeglang

To make the most of your visit to DM Tirta Pandeglang, here are a few tips:

  • Plan your visit during the week to avoid crowds.
  • Arrive early to beat the heat and avoid long queues.
  • Wear comfortable clothing and shoes.
  • Bring sunscreen and a hat to protect yourself from the sun.
  • Keep your belongings safe and secure.

Conclusion

DM Tirta Pandeglang is a great destination for those who love hot springs and natural beauty. With its affordable ticket prices, the resort offers a relaxing and rejuvenating experience for visitors of all ages. By following the tips provided in this article, you can make the most of your visit to DM Tirta Pandeglang and enjoy a fun-filled day with your family and friends.
