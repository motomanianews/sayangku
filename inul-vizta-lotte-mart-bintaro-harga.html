Inul Vizta Lotte Mart Bintaro Harga - Indonesian Lifestyle at Its Finest

Inul Vizta Lotte Mart Bintaro is a popular shopping destination in Jakarta, Indonesia, offering a unique blend of international and local products. Located in the heart of Bintaro, this modern mall provides an unparalleled shopping experience for its customers. In this article, we will delve into the details of Inul Vizta Lotte Mart Bintaro, including its prices and reviews.

Overview

Inul Vizta Lotte Mart Bintaro is a 3-storey shopping mall that offers a wide range of products, from food and beverages to fashion and beauty. The mall is divided into several zones, each featuring different types of stores and services. With over 200 stores and kiosks, customers can find everything they need under one roof.

Zones at Inul Vizta Lotte Mart Bintaro

Food Court

The food court at Inul Vizta Lotte Mart Bintaro offers a diverse selection of international and local cuisines. From Japanese and Korean to Indonesian and Western food, there's something for everyone. The food court is also air-conditioned, making it a comfortable spot to dine and relax.

Fashion Zone

The fashion zone at Inul Vizta Lotte Mart Bintaro is a shopper's paradise. With brands such as Guardian, Starbucks, and Korea's popular beauty brand, Laneige, customers can find the latest trends and styles. From casual wear to formal attire, the fashion zone has everything to make you look and feel great.

Beauty & Wellness Zone

The beauty and wellness zone at Inul Vizta Lotte Mart Bintaro is designed to cater to customers' beauty and wellness needs. With stores offering skincare, hair care, and makeup services, customers can pamper themselves with a range of treatments. The zone also includes a yoga studio and a fitness center.
