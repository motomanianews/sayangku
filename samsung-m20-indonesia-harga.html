Introduced to Indonesia: Samsung Galaxy M20

Samsung, one of the largest smartphone manufacturers in the world, has finally introduced its latest mid-range series, Galaxy M20, to the Indonesian market. This device aims to provide high-quality features at an affordable price, making it a suitable option for those who want a powerful phone without breaking the bank.

Harga Samsung Galaxy M20 Indonesia

Before we dive into the details of this device, let's talk about the price. The Samsung Galaxy M20 is available in two storage variants: 3GB RAM+ 32GB storage and 4GB RAM+ 64GB storage. The prices of these variants are IDR 2,999,000 and IDR 3,299,000 respectively.

Spesifikasi Samsung Galaxy M20

Here are the key specifications of the Samsung Galaxy M20:

  • Display: 6.3-inch FHD+ Infinity-V Display
  • Processor: Exynos 7904 Octa-core processor
  • RAM: 3GB or 4GB
  • Storage: 32GB or 64GB
  • Expandable Storage: Up to 512GB via microSD card
  • Camera: Dual rear cameras (13MP + 5MP), 8MP front camera
  • Battery: 5000mAh
  • Operating System: Android 9.0 (Pie)
  • Connectivity: 4G, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth 5.0, USB-C
  • Dimensions: 159.7 x 75.1 x 8.8mm
  • Weight: 186 grams

Review Samsung Galaxy M20 Indonesia

The Samsung Galaxy M20 is a solid device that checks all the right boxes. The display is vibrant and offers good viewing angles, the processor is efficient, and the camera performance is impressive. The 5000mAh battery lasts a full day and more, making it suitable for heavy users.

One of the standout features of the Galaxy M20 is its Infinity-V Display, which provides an immersive viewing experience. The device also runs smoothly, thanks to the efficient Exynos 7904 processor.

In terms of camera performance, the Galaxy M20 takes good low-light photos, and the 8MP front camera is sufficient for selfies and video calls. The device also supports fast charging, which is a welcome feature.

Kelebihan dan Kekurangan Samsung Galaxy M20 Indonesia

Here are some of the pros and cons of the Samsung Galaxy M20:

Kelebihan:

  • Good display quality
  • Efficient processor
  • Impressive camera performance
  • Long-lasting battery life
  • Fast charging support

Kekurangan:

  • No IP68 water and dust resistance
  • No headphone jack
  • No wireless charging support

Kesimpulan

The Samsung Galaxy M20 is a solid device that offers a great balance of performance and value. With its impressive display, efficient processor, and long-lasting battery life, it's a great option for those who want a powerful phone without breaking the bank.
